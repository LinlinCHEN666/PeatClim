This folder is intentionally distributed without preindustrial climate data and land–sea masks from HadCM3BL model.

Information on the tdezc simulation can be found at:
https://www.paleo.bristol.ac.uk/ummodel/data/tdezc/standard_new_html/tdezc.html

General information on the BRIDGE simulation archive is available at:
https://www.paleo.bristol.ac.uk/resources/simulations/

For guidance on navigating the archive and accessing data, see:
Using_BRIDGE_webpages.pdf, especially Section 5, “Accessing this Data”.

Citation: 
Valdes, P. J. et al. (2017). _The BRIDGE HadCM3 family of climate models: HadCM3@Bristol v1.0, Geosci_. Model Dev., 10, 3715–3743, https://doi.org/10.5194/gmd-10-3715-2017, 2017.
